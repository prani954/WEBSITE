Event - 2

IEEE ITSS Awareness Campaign/Seminar on Two-Wheeler Safety and Mobility Technologies

Hosted by Ahmedabad University, Gujarat

Date: 3rd and 4th November, 2023

About the event :

This campaign is intended to create awareness on the nature of accidents that happen while riding 2- wheelers. Importantly it will discuss and debate how accidents can be avoided in 2-wheelers by using simple yet powerful ICT-based technologies. This IEEE ITSSS sponsored symposium would be in blended mode and would be hosted by Ahmedabad University, Gujarat, India

For Event details visit our Brochure : Two wheeler Safety and Mobility

Speakers :

  • Prof. Mehul S Raval ( Chair Intelligent Transportation Systems Research Cluster, Ahmedabad University, Gujarat )
  • Dr. Hrishikesh Venkataraman (Head Smart Transportation Research Group, IIIT Sri City, AP)
  • Dr. Maryam Kaveshgar (Member Intelligent Transportation Systems Research Cluster, Ahmedabad University, Gujarat)
